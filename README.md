# 💰 Business Intelligence Dashboard
### $2.2M+ Retail Sales & Profit Analysis Using Excel

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Built an interactive Superstore Sales Dashboard using 
Microsoft Excel to analyze retail sales data across 
regions, customer segments, and product categories.

---

## 🎯 Key Highlights

- 📊 Analyzed **$22,97,200** in total revenue and 
  **$2,86,397** in total profit

- 🗂️ Created **3 dashboard views:**
  - Overview
  - Products Analytics
  - Customer Analysis

- 🏆 Identified **Top 5 & Bottom 5 products**
  — Binders (#1 with 5,974 orders)

- 🗺️ Discovered **West region** as highest 
  revenue driver ($7,25,457)

- 👥 **Consumer segment** leads sales at $11,61,401

- 🥇 **Sean Miller** identified as top customer 
  at $25,043

---

## 💡 Key Insight

> The West region drives the highest revenue, and 
> the Consumer segment dominates sales at $11.6L 
> — peak sales hit every **October to December!**
